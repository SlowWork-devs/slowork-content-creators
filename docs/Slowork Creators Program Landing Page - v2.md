## **2\. Story section — Why this exists**

**H2**  
 **Remote work was supposed to feel freer than this**

**Body copy**  
 Somewhere along the way, remote work became just another version of the same lifestyle.  
 Same stress. Same noise. Same burnout. Just with a laptop.

Slowork was created as a response to that.

We believe remote work should feel more human.  
 More connected to place.  
 More connected to people.  
 More aligned with the way you actually want to live.

That is why we are building Slowork: a platform for discovering remote-ready stays in meaningful environments, with community, experiences and a slower rhythm built in.

## **3\. What Slowork is**

**H2**  
 **What is Slowork?**

**Body copy**  
 Slowork is not just another booking app.

It is a platform for remote workers and digital nomads who want more than good WiFi.  
 They want places that support deep work.  
 Natural environments that improve how they feel.  
 Experiences that help them disconnect well.  
 And a sense of community while they travel.

In practical terms, Slowork helps people discover remote-ready stays, organise their trip, explore experiences nearby and connect with others through the app.

**Closing line**  
 It is a platform, yes.  
 But more importantly, it is a lifestyle proposition.

## **4\. This is not a typical creator program**

**H2**  
 **This is not a typical travel creator program**

**Body copy**  
 Most creator programs are built around campaigns, deliverables and transactional promotion.

This one is different.

Slowork is not looking for creators who can post the most.  
 We are looking for creators who can represent something meaningful.

This program exists to build a layer of organic, aspirational content around a different way of living and working remotely, one shaped by nature, focus, creativity, wellbeing and community.

If your content is driven by forced promotion, fast ads or generic travel aesthetics, this probably will not fit.

If your content feels thoughtful, visual, intentional and aligned with a slower rhythm of life, you are exactly who this was designed for.
