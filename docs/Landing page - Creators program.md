## **Section 1**

### H2 This is not a typical travel content creator program

Most creator programs are built around campaigns, deliverables and transactional content.

This one is different.

Slowork’s content creator program is not designed to reward volume, aggressive promotion or generic travel posts. Its purpose is to build a layer of organic, aspirational content that reflects the Slowork lifestyle: nature, focus, community and a more intentional rhythm of life .

If your content is built around quick ads and forced promotion, this probably won’t fit. If your content is thoughtful, visual and aligned with a slower way of living and working, you’re in the right place.

## **Section 2** 

### H2 What is Slowork

Slowork is not a booking platform in the usual sense. That positioning would make it a commodity, and that is exactly what Slowork should avoid.

Slowork is built around identity. It exists for people who work remotely and want more than good WiFi. They want:

* calmer environments  
* meaningful community  
* spaces that support deep work  
* a lifestyle that feels aligned with who they are

That is why this content creator program is not about selling stays. It is about helping represent a way of living that people already aspire to.

## **Section 3**

### H2 How the content creator program works

The system is simple.

#### H3 1\. Create content

You publish content on Instagram, TikTok or YouTube.

#### **H3 2\. Mention Slowork correctly**

Your content must include a tag, collaboration mention, link or clear reference to Slowork .

#### **H3 3\. Submit it for review**

You upload the piece through the creator portal.

#### **H3 4\. We review it**

Every submission is checked manually based on visual quality, brand alignment, originality and narrative value .

#### **H3 5\. Earn credits**

If the content is approved, credits are assigned to your account.

## **Section 4**

#### H2 What kind of content gets approved

We are not looking for content that pushes a product.

We are looking for content that inspires, connects and reflects the Slowork lifestyle.

#### H3 Content we want to see

* aspirational POVs  
* city vs nature contrasts  
* slow work routines  
* remote work in meaningful environments  
* personal experiences with real narrative  
* calm, visual and intentional storytelling

#### H3 Content that won’t be approved

* direct app promotion  
* empty or generic content  
* spammy posts  
* low-effort content  
* giveaway-style content  
* language focused on “making money” or hard selling

This is a quality-first program. Mediocre content should not be approved, and volume is not the goal.

## **Section 5**

#### H2 Credits system for creators

Slowork creators do not earn cash per post. They earn credits.

Each credit has an internal value of 1 USD, but credits are not money and can only be used within Slowork . They are designed as access to the experience, not as a payment mechanism.

#### H3 Instagram credits

* Story: 1 credit  
* Post or carousel: 2 credits  
* Reel: 5 credits

#### H3 TikTok credits

* Story: 1 credit  
* Post or carousel: 1 credit  
* Short video: 3 credits

#### H3 YouTube credits

* Shorts: 2 credits  
* Short video (8–40 min): 3 credits  
* Long video or podcast (+40 min): 10 credits  
* Post or carousel: 2 credits

#### H3 Bonus credits

You may earn extra credits for:

* high reach: \+2 credits for 50k+ views  
* strong engagement: \+2 credits for notable comments, reposts, saves or shares

# **FAQ SEO-ready**

## **H2 Frequently asked questions**

#### H3 Do content creators get paid by Slowork?

No. Slowork creators earn credits, not direct payment. Credits can be used within the platform and cover up to 50% of a transaction .

#### H3 How do I earn credits in the Slowork creator program?

You earn credits by publishing content on Instagram, TikTok or YouTube, submitting it for review, and getting it approved based on quality, alignment and storytelling .

#### H3 What kind of content does Slowork approve?

Slowork approves content that represents its lifestyle: calm, focus, nature, community and intentional remote work. Generic promotion, spammy posts and low-effort content are not approved .

#### H3 Can I join if I am not a full-time digital nomad?

Potentially yes. The key factor is not the label but whether your content and lifestyle genuinely align with the Slowork ethos.

#### H3 Which platforms can I use?

Instagram, TikTok and YouTube are currently supported in the creator program .

#### H3 What happens if I delete approved content?

If approved content is deleted within 3 months, the assigned credits can be removed
