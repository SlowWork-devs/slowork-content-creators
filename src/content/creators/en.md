## What is Slowork?

Slowork is not just another booking app.

It is a platform for remote workers and digital nomads who want more than good WiFi. They want places that support deep work. Natural environments that improve how they feel. Experiences that help them disconnect well. And a sense of community while they travel.

In practical terms, Slowork helps people discover remote-ready stays, organise their trip, explore experiences nearby and connect with others through the app.

It is a platform, yes. But more importantly, it is a lifestyle proposition.

## This is not a typical travel creator program

Most creator programs are built around campaigns, deliverables and transactional promotion. This one is different.

Slowork is not looking for creators who can post the most. We are looking for creators who can represent something meaningful.

This program exists to build a layer of organic, aspirational content around a different way of living and working remotely, one shaped by nature, focus, creativity, wellbeing and community.

If your content is driven by forced promotion, fast ads or generic travel aesthetics, this probably will not fit.

If your content feels thoughtful, visual, intentional and aligned with a slower rhythm of life, you are exactly who this was designed for.

## Why become a Slowork creator

Because not every collaboration helps you create work you are proud of.

Slowork creators are not here just to promote a product. They are here to document a lifestyle people already aspire to.

## Who this program is for

We are currently looking for creators in spaces like travel, lifestyle, digital nomad, remote work, intentional living and wellbeing.

Follower count is not the main filter. What matters more is whether your content feels relevant, visually strong and genuinely aligned with the Slowork world.

We are especially interested in creators who create in English or subtitle in English, already document meaningful environments, know how to tell stories—not just post aesthetics—and create content native to Instagram, TikTok or YouTube.

## What great Slowork content looks like

We are not looking for content that sells hard. We are looking for content that makes people feel something.

**Content we want:** aspirational POVs, remote work in nature, city vs nature contrasts, slow routines, meaningful storytelling, reflections about work, life and environment.

**Content that won’t be approved:** direct app promotion, generic travel montages, spammy content, low-effort posts, giveaway-style content, money-first messaging.

## How the creators program works

The system is intentionally simple.

1. Create content  
2. Mention Slowork clearly  
3. Submit it for review  
4. We review it manually  
5. Earn credits  

## If your content already reflects this way of life, you may be a fit

Slowork is being built right now. The creators who join early will not just promote it—they will help define what it becomes.

Create content that feels aligned. Earn your way into the experience. Help represent a better version of remote work.
